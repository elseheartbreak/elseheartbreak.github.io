A computer game about love, friendship and technology in a place where bits have replaces atoms

Introduction

Sebastian has just landed his first job in the distant city of Dorisburg. He moves there to start his adult life and figure out who he really wants to be. Among a strange collection of people, hackers and activists he finds some true friends – perhaps even love. But can they stop the terrible doings of the people ruling the city? And who will get their heart broken in the end?

Trailer

Features

  • An expansive 3d world ready to be explored in as many dimensions as you can find
  • Many hours of engaging story inside a complete and masterly simulated world, with people that feel truly alive
  • A true role playing game where your actions matter and nothing can ever be unsaid or undone
  • Tons of every-day objects and computers that works and can be modified
  • A chill experience where you can just go to the café and have a coffee whenever things get too hectic
